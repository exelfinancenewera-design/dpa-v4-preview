The State of Down Payment Assistance in 2026

The data buyers, agents and lenders keep asking for — programs, amounts, and how far they go. Free to cite.

Key figure: As of Q1 2026 there are 2,679 down payment assistance programs in the United States, and 77% are active and funded (source: Down Payment Resource, Q1 2026 program count). They range from outright grants to deferred, forgivable second loans — most cover part or all of the 3% conventional (or 3.5% FHA) minimum down payment.

How affordability is calculated

Affordability uses the FHA front-end (31% of gross monthly income) and back-end (43% including debts) DTI guidelines, solved against a 30-year amortization, with property tax and insurance estimated at national averages (1.1% / 0.35%). Assistance is applied against a 3% minimum down payment. Try it with your own numbers in the calculator.
