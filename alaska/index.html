What down payment assistance is available in Alaska?

Alaska's main statewide option is the AHFC Affordable Housing Enhanced Loan (AHEL) — interest-rate / assistance program administered by the state housing finance agency. Many cities and counties in Alaska add their own programs on top, so it is worth checking all three levels.

How much house can I afford in Alaska with assistance?

It depends on your income, debts and credit. The calculator applies the FHA 31%/43% debt-to-income rule to estimate your price, then shows how much of your down-payment gap Alaska assistance could cover. Most buyers pair it with a 3% conventional or 3.5% FHA down payment.
