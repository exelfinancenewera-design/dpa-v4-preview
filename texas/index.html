Texas DPA — common questions

What down payment assistance is available in Texas?

Texas's main statewide option is the TSAHC Down Payment Assistance Grants — Grants of up to ~4-5% of the loan amount; exact % varies by program tier. It is a grant (non-repayable). Cities and counties in Texas often add their own programs on top.
