Ohio DPA — common questions

What down payment assistance is available in Ohio?

Ohio's main statewide option is the OHFA Your Choice! Down Payment Assistance — forgivable second loan administered by the state housing finance agency. Many cities and counties in Ohio add their own programs on top, so it is worth checking all three levels.

How much house can I afford in Ohio with assistance?

It depends on your income, debts and credit. The calculator applies the FHA 31%/43% debt-to-income rule to estimate your price, then shows how much of your down-payment gap Ohio assistance could cover. Most buyers pair it with a 3% conventional or 3.5% FHA down payment.

What credit score do I need for Ohio down payment assistance?

Many state programs set a 620 minimum credit score alongside income limits based on area median income. Exact requirements are set by the agency — verify current terms on the official OHFA Your Choice! Down Payment Assistance page before applying.
